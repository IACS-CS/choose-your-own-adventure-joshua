Inner Thoughts

"I think I'm gonna wait out here." Cameron said, feeling he was making the right choice.

"Alright, I'll meet you out here when I'm done." Said Lucious, in a calm tone.

Cameron sat on a bench outside the piuub, thinking about what had happened to that day.

  • He couldn't win a fight despite his training.
  • His sensei had been kidnapped by a maniac.
  • His home was set on eternal fire.

Overall he just felt down, knowing he probably could have done more. "And I thought I was depressing." Said a joking Lucious from behind him.

"It's been a rough day." Cameron responded.

Cam closed his eyes to think some more when he felt smooth darksteel in his hands. He looked down to see a sleek shiny broadsword resting on his hands. "What do you think?" Lucious asked.

Cam clutched the hilt of the sword in his right hand, stood up, and turned to lucios. "Let's go kill some dragons."
