Chapter 1: The Great Underground

It was a peaceful day in the village of Crater Island. But in the monostary on Peace Mountain, our heroes were hard at work, training for a battle no one wanted to come. Cameron(you) was attacking a dummy with a dulled stone broadsword, while Lucious hacked through 10-20 training dummies violently. "You keep that up, we're gonna have to replace them again," Cameron joked with his partner.

"That's the plan," Lucious responded in a serious tone. "Never let your gaurd down."

Cam turned back to his dummy, when a loud slam on the door was heard "Who's there?", Lucious demanded.

As Lucious drew his sword, the pair approached the door, Cameron trying to hide his fear. The door burst open, revealing 3 earth dragon soldiers. "Lucious!" the dragon in the center snarled. "Hand over the old man!"

"I would, but I take it you have ill intentions," Lucious asserted with confidence. "Go tell your boss the war is over, and you lost."

"We would, and we did. And like a brat who loses despite cheating, he demands a second game of cards." the dragon rebuttled.

"That analogy is truer than you're willing to admit." Lucious said.

"You, get the boy," the creature growled to the dragon on his left, before turning to his right. "You, get to the door. I'll deal with him."

The dragon on the left turned to Cameron and drew his sword. Cam considered his options. He could either use his training to try to fight, or he could run and warn sensei. What do you do?
