Draggino's Pub

"I'm gonna head in." Cameron stated with confidence.

"Wise choice." Lucious said. "Maybe he'll let you get a custom mold. But it'll cost extra."

The two enter the building and walk to the counter, where a green ooze dragon is shining a plate. "Hey, Draggino." Lucious aid.

The dragon turned around, with a big toothy grin on his face. "What's up, guys? The usual?" He questioned.

"Actually, we'd like to order something off the menu." Lucious said in a sly tone. "It's his first time."

"I see." Draggino siad, dropping his friendly tone. "Follow me."

The three headed to the back room, which was lined with swords. Draggino walked over to a silver broadsword, picked it up, and laid it out on the table. "This will work well for beginners." Draggino said. "Though I must ask what's the occasion?"

"Pyro." Cameron siad, wanting revenge desperately.

"The council took sensei." Lucious said, feeling rage for what happened.

"I see," Draggino siad. "Well, if you need anything, you can come to me."

"Thanks," Lucious said, before turning to hsi partner. "You ready?"

"Let's kill some dragons." Cameron said, clutching his new blade for the first time, filling him with confidence.
